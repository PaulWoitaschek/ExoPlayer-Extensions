      • seek

        void seek()
        Notifies the reader that a seek has occurred.

        Following a call to this method, the data passed to the next invocation of consume(com.google.android.exoplayer2.util.ParsableByteArray, int) will not be a continuation of the data that was previously passed. Hence the reader should reset any internal state.
